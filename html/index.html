About Us

Urbanova is more than just a shopping destination — it's a lifestyle hub designed to inspire, entertain, and connect people from all walks of life. Built with a vision to blend elegance, comfort, and diversity under one roof, we are redefining how modern malls function in today’s fast-paced world. From globally renowned fashion brands to exclusive local artisans, from gourmet restaurants to cozy cafés — every element of Urbanova is thoughtfully selected to elevate your experience.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond shopping. We host regular community events, seasonal festivals, and cultural showcases, creating a dynamic space that feels alive throughout the year. With eco-conscious infrastructure, smart navigation systems, safe and secure premises, and family-friendly zones, Urbanova ensures a seamless experience for all age groups.

At Urbanova, we believe in creating moments that matter. Whether you're visiting to unwind, explore, celebrate, or simply pass time, you'll find a warm atmosphere that welcomes you every step of the way. We’re proud to be your neighborhood mall and your weekend getaway — all in one beautiful space.
